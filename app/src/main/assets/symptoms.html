Common symptoms of COVID-19 include:
  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Loss of taste or smell

Other symptoms can include sore throat, muscle aches, headaches, congestion, and gastrointestinal symptoms like diarrhea or nausea. Keep in mind that symptoms can range from mild to severe, and some people may be asymptomatic (show no symptoms at all). If you suspect COVID-19, it's important to get tested and follow local health guidelines.
